About Us

Welcome to CampusCloset

CampusCloset is a second-hand marketplace exclusively for students. We aim to create a sustainable ecosystem where students can buy, sell, and exchange pre-loved goods within their campus community.

Our platform allows students to save money, reduce waste, and give unused items a new home. Whether you are looking for textbooks, electronics, furniture, or even clothing, CampusCloset makes it easy and safe to connect with fellow students.

Join us in our mission to make student life more affordable and environmentally friendly!
